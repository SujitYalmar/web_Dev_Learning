Creamy Chickpea and Herb Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped

Recipe

  1. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until soft and fragrant.
  2. Stir in the turmeric and diced potatoes, cooking for another 5 minutes.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm
